Together we can make a difference

By working together and raising funds to support cat-welfare and cat rescue charities we can make a difference to improve the welfare and care of cats and to find loving homes for them to help their owners have happier more fulfilling lives. By supporting us and telling your friends about us you will be making a huge difference. If you’d like to know more about how you can help please get in touch.
